Interview Patterns using Heap

Heaps are heavily used in:

Top K elements
K-way merge
Median stream
Scheduling
Greedy + Heap
Graph algorithms
Dijkstra's algorithm
Prim's algorithm

When interviewer asks why heap?, answer:

For Top K:

“Heap lets us maintain the best k candidates efficiently in O(log k).”

For scheduling:

“Heap gives quick access to the highest priority task.”

For streams:

“Heap supports dynamic insertions while preserving order.”
