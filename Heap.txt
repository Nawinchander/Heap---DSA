What is a Heap?

A Heap is a complete binary tree that satisfies the heap property.

Min Heap:

Parent is smaller than children.

        2
      /   \
     5     8
    / \   /
   10 7 15

Max Heap:

Parent is greater than children.

        20
      /    \
    15      10
   /  \
  8    7

Array Representation

Heap is stored in an array:

[2, 5, 8, 10, 7, 15]

For index i:

left child = 2*i + 1
right child = 2*i + 2
parent = Math.floor((i-1)/2)
