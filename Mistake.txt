Common Heap Interview Mistakes
Mistake 1:

Sorting instead of heap

O(n log n)

Instead:

O(n log k)
Mistake 2:

Using wrong heap

kth largest → min heap
kth smallest → max heap

Mistake 3:

Not explaining heap size logic

The interviewer wants:

“Heap size remains k, so insertion/removal is log k.”

Heap Interview Summary

For FAANG interviews, heaps are expected in:

Top K
Priority Scheduling
Streaming
Greedy
Graphs

Master these patterns:

Min heap for top K
Two heaps for median
Heap for scheduling
Heap for merging K streams

These patterns cover many heap interview problems at Amazon and Meta.
